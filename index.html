Who are we?

SellYourComicCollection is a family run business that specialises in the collecting, buying, and selling, of comic books. Oh, another one? I hear you say? Actually no.

The reason our business exists, and what sets us apart from the others, is that we are, at heart, and before all else, collectors. We're not here to make a quick buck and move the books on to the next for a quick profit, we're here because of our love of the industry, the collection game itself, and, of course, the comics. Because of this, you can trust that we will not just be offering fair prices for your treasured comic books, but if they don't fit our own collecting criteria, each and every one will be going to people that care for them as much as you once did. With our extensive customer base, we make sure this happens.

Why choose us?

Well, this parts easy. Have you ever tried to sell a comic collection? Or even just a few comics? If so, you already know the answer to this.

Comic books are Notoriously difficult to shift, be it ebay, facebook or wherever you choose to list your little stack of treasures. Selling just a few comic books could be months worth of work in itself, not to mention the time taken to research proper selling titles, learn comic book grade assesments (an absolute must), and research which print your book is, or even a place or platform to sell, plus countless other annoying bits that end up ultimately being more time than its worth, especially due to the fact that the average comic book isn't worth much more than the paper it's printed on. On top of this all, there's the unfortunate fact that in this modern day and age, all unprotected transactions run the risk of fraud, or being scammed. There is a LOT of these scams out there, many focusing specifically on people selling items such as these, so please - even if you choose not to take us up on our offer, ensure that you do your research on the techniques that scammers use beforehand.

How to request a quote?

This parts easy. First, decide wether you're selling a small stack of individual issues, or a large collection.

If you find yourself at odds with a bulk load - Just contact us directly, either via the contact form above, or via our email INEEDTOCREATEANEMAIL@HOTMAIL.COM. Things to note if taking this route that would speed this process along, would be the name of publishers within the collection, estimate issue count, age, and condition of the lot.

Or if its single, or a low ammount of comic books - Photograph each individual cover, whilst trying to document any defects or damage to the book. Moneys are not paid until the book has been recieved, but we are able to give an accurate pricing prior to sending, provided the images sent are accurate.
